# Comparing Numbers

When two numbers are different, one is bigger and one is smaller.

We use these signs:

- `>` means **greater than**
- `<` means **less than**
- `=` means **equal to**

<Callout type="info" title="Remember">
  The open side of the sign always points to the **bigger** number.
</Callout>

## Examples

- 7 **>** 3 (seven is greater than three)
- 2 **<** 9 (two is less than nine)
- 5 **=** 5 (five equals five)
