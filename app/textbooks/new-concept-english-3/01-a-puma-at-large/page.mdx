## The Text 课文

Pumas are large, cat-like animals which are found in America. When reports came into London Zoo that a wild puma had been spotted forty-five miles south of London, they were not taken seriously. However, as the evidence began to accumulate, experts from the Zoo felt obliged to investigate, for the descriptions given by people who claimed to have seen the puma were extraordinarily similar.

The hunt for the puma began in a small village where a woman picking blackberries saw 'a large cat' only five yards away from her. It immediately ran away when she saw it, and experts confirmed that a puma will not attack a human being unless it is cornered. The search proved difficult, for the puma was often observed at one place in the morning and at another place twenty miles away in the evening. Wherever it went, it left behind it a trail of dead deer and small animals like rabbits. Paw prints were seen in a number of places and puma fur was found clinging to bushes. Several people complained of 'cat-like noises' at night and a businessman on a fishing trip saw the puma up a tree. The experts were now fully convinced that the animal **was** a puma, but where had it come from? As no pumas had been reported missing from any zoo in the country, this one must have been in the possession of a private collector and somehow managed to escape. The hunt went on for several weeks, but the puma was not caught. It is disturbing to think that a dangerous wild animal is still at large in the quiet countryside.

## 3. 关键句型 Simple / Compound / Complex

<Callout type="tip" title="一分钟回顾">
  - **Simple 简单句**：一个主谓结构。*The hunt went on for several weeks.*
  - **Compound 并列句**：两个独立分句用 **and / but / or / so / for / yet** 连起来。*Paw prints were seen in a number of places **and** puma fur was found clinging to bushes.*
  - **Complex 复合句**：含至少一个从句（when / because / who / which / that / if / as / where 等引导）。*It immediately ran away **when** she saw it.*

  注意：**for** 在书面英语里可以当"等于 because"的并列连词使用——这是本课的高级点。
</Callout>

## 4. 难点：句末介词 Sentence-ending prepositions

<Callout type="tip" title="一句话讲透">
  过去有种规定说"英文不能以介词结尾"，**现代英语早已不遵守**这条禁忌。除了最正式的书面语，把介词留在句尾远比 *About whom...?* 这种古板写法自然。

  - ❌ *About whom are you talking?*
  - ✅ *Who(m) are you talking **about**?*

  课文中 *where had it come **from**?*（l.14）就是地道用法。
</Callout>

## 9. 摘要写作 Summary writing (≤ 80 词)

<Callout type="info" title="任务">
  In not more than **80 words**, describe how experts came to the conclusion that the animal seen by many people really was a puma. Do not include anything that is not in the passage.

  按下面 9 个提示点连成一段：

  1. What sort of reports were received by London Zoo?
  2. Were the reports similar in nature or not?
  3. Who saw it first?
  4. Did it stay in one place, or did it move from place to place?
  5. What did it leave behind it?
  6. Were paw prints and puma fur found as well or not?
  7. What was heard at night?
  8. Was the animal seen up a tree or not?
  9. Were experts now sure that the animal really was a puma or not?
</Callout>

<Callout type="tip" title="参考范文（自己写完再看）">
  Reports of a wild puma south of London were ignored at first, but as evidence accumulated, the descriptions proved extraordinarily similar. A woman picking blackberries saw it five yards away, and it ran from her — pumas attack only when cornered. The animal moved twenty miles overnight, leaving a trail of dead deer and rabbits. Paw prints and tufts of fur were found; cat-like noises were heard at night; a businessman even saw it up a tree. The experts were now fully convinced. *(80 words)*
</Callout>
